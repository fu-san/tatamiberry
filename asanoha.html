Asanoha

麻の葉

❀   ❀   ❀
Meaning

The hemp leaf pattern is a representative auspicious pattern in Japan, symbolizing the wish for the healthy growth and longevity of children, drawing inspiration from the rapid growth and strength of hemp, which can reach 4 meters in just 4 months.

Wish

★ May my child grow up healthy and strong...　★ May they be protected from illness and misfortune...
