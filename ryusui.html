Ryusui

流水

∿   ∿   ∿
Meaning

The flowing water pattern is a classic Japanese auspicious motif that represents gently moving, winding streams. It has long been associated with purification, protection, and wishes for longevity.

Because water flows continuously without stopping, it also symbolizes harmony in relationships and lasting prosperity. This elegant design is widely used throughout the year in kimono, yukata, and various traditional items.

Purification and Protection
Water is seen as a symbol of cleansing, believed to wash away impurities, misfortune, and negative energy.

Eternity and Prosperity
The uninterrupted flow of water represents a life of longevity, harmonious relationships, and enduring happiness.

Wish

May your life flow gently and freely, and may every change lead you to something beautiful.
