## 各文様の内容（英語テキスト）

> **構成ルール:** 各ページは **Meaning（意味）** と **Wish（願い）** の2セクション構成。
> **続きを読む機能:** 全ページに「Read more ▼ / Read less ▲」ボタンのCSS・JSを実装済み。テキストが短いページはボタン非表示、長いページのみ表示される。

### 🌊 Seigaiha（青海波）
- **Meaning:** An auspicious pattern depicting the appearance of endlessly spreading, gentle waves in a fan shape.
- **Wish:** May your life flow gently like endless waves.

### 🌿 Asanoha（麻の葉）
- **Meaning:** This pattern represents growth, protection, and healthy development.
- **Wish:** May you grow strong and shine in your own way.

### ⭕ Shippo（七宝）
- **Meaning:** This pattern represents harmony, connection, and endless relationships.
- **Wish:** May wonderful connections surround you always.

### ◼ Ichimatsu（市松）
- **Meaning:** This pattern represents prosperity and the hope for a bright future.
- **Wish:** May your future be filled with joy and success.

### 🌸 Sakura（桜）
- **Meaning:** This pattern represents beauty, new beginnings, and the cycle of life.
- **Wish:** May each day bring you new happiness.

### 🌿 Karakusa（唐草）
- **Meaning:** A pattern of vines growing endlessly. It symbolizes prosperity, longevity, and connections that last.
- **Wish:** May your connections continue to grow and bring lasting happiness.

### 🐢 Kikkou（亀甲）
- **Meaning:** A pattern inspired by a turtle's shell. It symbolizes longevity, good health, and protection.
- **Wish:** May you be blessed with good health and a long, happy life.

### 🏹 Yabane（矢羽根）
- **Meaning:** A pattern made of arrow feathers. It symbolizes growth, moving forward, and achieving goals.
- **Wish:** May you move forward with confidence toward your dreams and goals.

### 🐟 Uroko（鱗）
- **Meaning:** A pattern inspired by fish scales. It symbolizes protection, warding off evil, and new beginnings.
- **Wish:** May you be protected from harm and blessed with a bright new future.

### 🌼 Kiku（菊）
- **Meaning:** A pattern of chrysanthemum flowers. It symbolizes nobility, longevity, and wishing for happiness.
- **Wish:** May happiness continue in your life and your heart be always fulfilled.

### 🌸 Ume（梅）
- **Meaning:** The plum blossom is one of Japan's most beloved and auspicious symbols. It blooms bravely in the cold of winter, even before the snow has melted. Because of this, it represents resilience, hope, and the courage to begin again.
- **Wish:** May you bloom with courage, just like the plum blossom in winter.

### 🌲 Matsu（松）
- **Meaning:** The pine tree is a symbol of strength, long life, and unchanging loyalty. Because it stays green through even the harshest winters, it represents endurance, good fortune, and the spirit that never gives up.
- **Wish:** May you stand strong through every season of life, always growing and always green.

### 🎋 Daruma（達磨）
- **Meaning:** The Daruma doll is a beloved symbol of perseverance and good luck in Japan. （続きを読む機能あり）
- **Wish:** May you rise every time you fall, and never give up on your dreams.

### 🍃 Tonbo（とんぼ）
- **Meaning:** In Japan, the dragonfly is called kachimushi — the "victory insect." （続きを読む機能あり）
- **Wish:** May you move forward with purpose, and find victory in every step of your journey.

### 💧 Mizutama（水玉）
- **Meaning:** Water dots represent the gentle power of flowing water — pure, cleansing, and ever-moving. （続きを読む機能あり）
- **Wish:** May your life flow gently and freely, always finding its way.

### 🌊 Ryusui（流水）
- **Meaning:** Ryusui depicts the graceful movement of winding streams and flowing rivers. （続きを読む機能あり）
- **Wish:** May your life flow with grace, always finding its way forward, no matter what lies ahead.

## 新しい文様を追加するときのルール

1. **ファイル名:** 文様名の英語ローマ字（例: `uroko.html`, `kikkou.html`）
2. **select.html** のリストにも追加する（CSS + カードHTML）
3. **テキスト構成:** Meaning / Wish の **2セクション**で英語で書く（Messageは不要）
4. **続きを読む:** 長い説明文は `<span class="meaning-extra">` で囲む → ボタン自動表示
5. **デザイン:** 既存ページのスタイルを踏襲（カラー・レイアウト統一）
6. **文様画像:** くーちゃんがHTML内に空の丸型グラデーション円を用意 → ふうさんがCanvaで文様を追加
